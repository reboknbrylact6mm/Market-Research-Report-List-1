<p><strong>The Seatbelt Pretensioners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lap Pretensioners</li><li>Buckle Pretensioners</li><li>Retractor Pretensioners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The seatbelt pretensioners market consists of three primary types: lap pretensioners, buckle pretensioners, and retractor pretensioners. Lap pretensioners tighten the seatbelt across the lap during a collision, reducing occupant movement. Buckle pretensioners engage at the buckle point to pull the seatbelt tighter upon impact, enhancing safety. Retractor pretensioners are integrated into the seatbelt retractor mechanism and automatically tighten the belt when sensing a crash. Collectively, these technologies significantly improve passenger protection during accidents.</p></p>
