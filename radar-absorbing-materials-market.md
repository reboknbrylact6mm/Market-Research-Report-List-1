<p><p>The Radar Absorbing Materials (RAM) market is categorized into magnetic, dielectric, and hybrid types. Magnetic materials utilize ferromagnetic substances to dissipate radar waves through energy absorption. Dielectric materials, on the other hand, leverage their non-conductive properties to reduce radar signals by altering their permittivity. Hybrid materials combine features of both magnetic and dielectric types, optimizing absorption efficiency across a wider frequency range. Each type serves distinct applications, particularly in military stealth technology and electromagnetic compatibility.</p></p>
