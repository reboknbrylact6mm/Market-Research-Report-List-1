<p><p>The marine gear oil market is primarily categorized into mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil, derived from refining crude oil, offers good lubrication and is cost-effective but may have limitations in performance under extreme conditions. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is artificially engineered to provide superior protection, resistance to thermal breakdown, and improved efficiency in various marine environments. These differentiating factors influence the choice of oil based on specific operational needs, performance requirements, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
